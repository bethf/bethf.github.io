Read This! - Recommended Book Lists

Books for Upper Elementary and Middle School Level Readers

I love getting recommendations from students. Best of all, students love sharing books they love. Here is a list of books that 6th graders at our school recommend as the best books they read this year.

Alex Rider series, by Anthony Horowitz
Wolf Brother, by Michael Paver
Hunger Games, by Suzanne Collins
Young James Bond series (starting with SilverFin), by Charlie Higson
Ender's Game, by Orson Scott Card
Maze Runner, by James Dashner
Dangerous Days of Daniel X, by James Patterson
Tentacles, by Roland Smith
Montmorency: Thief, Liar, Gentlman? by Eleanor Updale
Mister Monday (Keys to the Kingdom series), by Garth Nix
Among the Hidden, by Margaret Peterson Haddix
Elephant Run, by Roland Smith

Wee Free Men, by Terry Pratchett
